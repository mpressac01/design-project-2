History of Persona 4 Golden










Persona 4 Golden which was released in 2012 is the remake of the game Persona 4 which was released in 2008. With Persona games most of the time there is a remake later on for example Persona 3 got Persona 3 Portable or Persona 3 Reload, Persona 5 got Persona 5 Royal and Persona 4 is now also getting Persona 4 Revival which is estimated to be released in February 2027. In most Persona remakes they add a new or a few new endings for example in Persona 4 Golden they added the Golden Ending. Basically in every remake they add new features which actually impact the personality of the story all together.
