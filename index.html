Welcome to Inaba a small rural town in Japan. You've just moved after your parents had a buissness related work move but fret not! You'll be living with your Uncle Ryotaro Dojima and your cousin Nanako Dojima. You will also start school at Yasogami High as the New Kid, but quickly things start to go wrong. A woman's body was found dangling of a telephone pole... And an old weird man starts to talk to you in your dreams calling himself Igor and that you are now in the Velvet Room. You must gain the social links of others in your school to figure out the mystery of Inaba.

























































Harness the powers of your different Personas as the Wild Card Arcana and befriend others in Inaba to strengthen your bonds and powers.
